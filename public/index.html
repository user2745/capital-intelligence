About

Independent intelligence. No product to sell.

The Capital Intelligence is an independent macro research publication focused exclusively on the intersection of four of the world's most important private wealth jurisdictions — the United States, Israel, the Gulf States, and Singapore.

Our readers are principals, CIOs, and advisors managing significant cross-border wealth who need analysis that reflects the complexity of their actual portfolios — not research filtered through a bank's product agenda.

We have no asset management arm, no proprietary funds, and no advertisers paying to influence our views. Our only obligation is to the quality of the intelligence we produce.
